WELCOME

The PCCOE Pune Placement Cell plays a vital role in connecting Undergraduates and Postgraduates with job opportunities by maintaining strong relationships with reputed companies and industrial establishments. The Placement Cell works throughout the year to facilitate interactions between companies and graduates, ensuring that students have access to valuable career opportunities. The number of students successfully placed through campus recruitment drives is consistently increasing, reflecting the growing success of the placement efforts at PCCOE Pune.

OBJECTIVES

  • 1. Developing the students to meet the Industries recruitment process

  • 2. To motivate students to develop Technical knowledge and soft skills in terms of career planning, goal setting

  • 3. To motivate students aspire for higher studies and guiding them to take competitive exams such as CAT, GATE, TOEFL, GRE, IES, UPSC, TNPSC etc

  • 4. Aiming to Place the maximum number of students through campus & off-campus interviews conducted by the top companies
